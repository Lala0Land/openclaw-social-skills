## Top 10 Skills for Social Media

1. **Bird** - X/Twitter CLI for reading, searching, and posting. Perfect for quick tweets or threads.  
   [Link](https://github.com/openclaw/skills/tree/main/skills/steipete/bird)

2. **Tweet-Writer** - Crafts viral, engaging tweets and threads. Ideal for hooking your audience.  
   [Link](https://github.com/openclaw/skills/tree/main/skills/sanky369/tweet-writer)

3. **Social-Content** - Helps create, schedule, and optimize posts across platforms. Your go-to for strategy.  
   [Link](https://github.com/openclaw/skills/tree/main/skills/jchopard69/marketing-skills/references/social-content)

4. **Postiz** - Schedules posts to 28+ channels (X, LinkedIn, Reddit, IG, TikTok, etc.). Set it and forget it!  
   [Link](https://github.com/openclaw/skills/tree/main/skills/nevo-david/postiz)

5. **Upload-Post** - Uploads content directly to TikTok, IG, YouTube, LinkedIn, FB, X, and more. Seamless sharing.  
   [Link](https://github.com/openclaw/skills/tree/main/skills/victorcavero14/upload-post)

6. **LinkedIn-CLI** - Searches profiles, checks messages, and automates LinkedIn interactions. Pro networking.  
   [Link](https://github.com/openclaw/skills/tree/main/skills/arun-8687/linkedin-cli)

7. **Reddit** - Browses, searches, posts, and moderates Reddit. Great for community building.  
   [Link](https://github.com/openclaw/skills/tree/main/skills/theglove44/reddit)

8. **Solobuddy** - Indie hacker's companion for Twitter engagement and content workflows. Build-in-public vibes.  
   [Link](https://github.com/openclaw/skills/tree/main/skills/humanji7/solobuddy)

9. **Gamma** - Generates AI-powered social posts, docs, and presentations. Visual content on steroids.  
   [Link](https://github.com/openclaw/skills/tree/main/skills/stopmoclay/gamma)

10. **Octolens** - Tracks brand mentions on Twitter, Reddit, GitHub, LinkedIn with sentiment analysis. Stay ahead of the buzz.  
    [Link](https://github.com/openclaw/skills/tree/main/skills/garrrikkotua/octolens)

## Installation & Usage
To use these in your Moltbot setup:
1. Clone the main skills repo: `git clone https://github.com/openclaw/skills`
2. Navigate to the specific skill folder.
3. Follow the skill's README for setup (usually involves copying to your bot's skills directory).
4. Run Moltbot and invoke the skill!

For more skills, check the full collection.
